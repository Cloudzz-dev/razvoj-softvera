# DFDS Branding Guidelines

## Voice & Tone
*   **Premium**: Confident, minimal, and high-quality.
*   **Direct**: No fluff. "Join the Network", not "Please sign up here".
*   **Visionary**: Focus on the future. "Build what's next."

## Typography
*   **Primary Font**: `Inter` (or `Geist Sans`).
*   **Headings**: Tight tracking (`tracking-tight`), bold weights.
*   **Body**: Relaxed tracking, readable weights (400/500).

## Color Palette

### Backgrounds
*   **Deep Zinc**: `bg-zinc-950` (#09090b) - Main background.
*   **Glass**: `bg-white/5` + `backdrop-blur-lg` - Panels and Cards.

### Accents
*   **Indigo Glow**: `from-indigo-500 to-purple-500` - Primary gradients.
*   **Success**: `emerald-500` - Growth/Investor metrics.
*   **Text**: `zinc-100` (Primary), `zinc-400` (Secondary).

## Glassmorphism Tokens
*   **Panel**: `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-lg border border-white/10 shadow-xl`
*   **Button**: `bg-white/10 hover:bg-white/20 active:scale-95 transition-all`

## Logo Concept
*   A simple, geometric "S" or "Start" icon.
*   Monochrome (White on Black) or subtle Indigo gradient.
